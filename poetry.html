Pick~a~Number Poetry Book

Welcome to the first version of my poetry book. Hope you enjoy!

I have written many poems~phrases~lyrics over the years and I wanted to avoid the inhumane act of endless scrolling.
This way, not only does it feel a little like a game, it is also a way to give each poem the individuality they deserve.
You will find that most are written in English, others in Spanish, and a few in Spanglish. Poems are not in chronological order :)
