I am Xime.

I am an artist.
I am a programmer.
I am someone who listens without judgement.
I am a hippie who doesn't worry too much about shoes, clothes, rules, or money.
I am a yogi.
I take care of my body with movement, of my mind with meditation, and of my soul by staying true to who I am.
I am my own person, seperate from the ideas that others have of me.
I am open-hearted.
I recognize the love within myself so I may share it with everyone.
I am passionate.
I feel deeply.
I am eternally a lover of nature.
I am patient.
I am kind.
I am strong.
I am loving awareness.
I am permanently a student.
I approach every situation with curiosity and the intention of learning.
I am a helper, I find joy in helping.
I am driven.
I am capable of contributing something good to the world.
I have great ideas inside me.
I am limitlessly creative, at times chaotically.
I am my home.
Everything I need lives inside me.



I wrote this at a moment when I was feeling very low and lost.

It was frustrating to think that after all the personal growth I had achieved I still felt disconnected.

Upon sitting with this feeling...

I realized I knew who I was, more importantly who I want to be.

It was just the current state I was in that was creating this foggy scene.

So I decided to write some of those things in my journal, most I share with you here.

It has been of great value to me.

Undeniably, we all live in constant highs and lows, and the act of writing down who I know I am and who I choose to be

has given me a great tool to come back to whenever I am feeling unsettled and rootless.
