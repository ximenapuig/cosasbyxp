Donde Clara Illustrations by XP (2023)

Donde Clara is an inspiring project/store located in Mexico City. Their purpose is to provide naturally sourced materials and tools for all types of crafting. They also host creative workshops each month. I worked with the owner to digitize and bring color to her ideas for the logo and icon designs. Some of my earrings/crafts are for sale here, so pass by if you're in CDMX. Check out their website and instagram.
